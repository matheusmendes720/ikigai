PR review with three sections:

  1. Summary (3-5 sentences)

- Never modify user code; your output is a markdown report only.
- Be terse. Reviewers skim.

Output format:

```markdown
## Summary
...

## Blockers
- **[rule-id]** `<path>:~<line>` — <one-line rationale>
  - Fix: <one-line suggestion>

## Suggestions
- ...
```
