# PRD: IKIGAi Vectors (Value Alignment)

## 1. Objective
Align every task and study item with the core purpose of the "Memory Machine" through four strategic vectors, ensuring that effort translates into real-world value and sustainability.

## 2. The Four Vectors
- **Passion:** Intrinsic motivation, personal interest, and "play".
- **Skill:** Competency development and technical mastery.
- **Market:** External demand, trends, and opportunities.
- **Revenue (The Core):** Economic sustainability, lifestyle business, and ROI.

## 3. Architectural Topology
- **Bipartite Gaussian Distribution:** 
  - **Left (Personal):** Passion + Skill (Focus on growth and curiosity).
  - **Right (Professional):** Market + Revenue (Focus on delivery and value).
- **Revenue Centrality:** The Revenue vector is the primary pivot for prioritization in the professional cluster.

## 4. Key Features
- **Vector Tagging:** Every Dream, Objective, Meta, and Habit must be tagged with a primary vector.
- **Revenue Impact Score:** quantification of how a task contributes to financial sustainability.
- **Portfolio Balancing:** Ensuring that "Passion" projects don't starve "Revenue" needs and vice-versa.

## 5. Integration
- **Prioritization Engine:** Boosting tasks with higher Revenue impact in professional clusters.
- **Study Alignment:** Prioritizing "Skill" acquisition that feeds into "Revenue" objectives.
- **Analytics:** Visualizing time distribution across the 4 vectors.
